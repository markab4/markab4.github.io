About Me



I am an undergrad student at Queens College, the City University of New York, in my last semester. In May, I will graduate with a Bachelors of Computer Science. I also minor in Interaction Design and Mathematics.

This semester I am taking the following courses: Natural Language Processing, Software Engineering, Information Design, Web Design II, and Information Design. I am also doing the CodePath Android Bootcamp, a twelve week project-focused Android training course including weekly projects and labs, as well as a collaborative course-wide group project.

My GitHub has many of my projects and design prototypes. Last summer, I interned at Sapient Razorfish, a digital technology consultancy, where I pushed production code and provided responsive front-end development for the Mercedes-Benz and Freightliner Vans websites.
